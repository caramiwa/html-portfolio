The Best Movies According to Mira

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Howl's Moving Castle


A castle powered by a fire demon. A cursed old woman. An eccentric young wizard who saw the woman through the curse.

The Edge of Tomorrow


Follows a soldier who, after dying in a battle with extraterrestrials, is caught in a time loop that makes him live the same day repeatedly, allowing him to improve his fighting skills.

To Wong Foo, Thanks for Everything! Julie Newmar


Three drag queens travel cross-country until their car breaks down, leaving them stranded in a small town.
